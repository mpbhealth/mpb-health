# App Store Assets Required for Deployment

## iOS App Store Requirements

### App Icon
- **Size**: 1024x1024px
- **Format**: PNG (no transparency)
- **Location**: `assets/images/icon.png`

### Screenshots Required
- iPhone 6.9" Display (2868x1320px) - at least 3 required
- iPhone 6.7" Display (2796x1290px) - at least 3 required
- iPhone 6.5" Display (2778x1284px) - at least 3 required
- iPhone 5.5" Display (2208x1242px) - at least 3 required
- iPad Pro 12.9" Display (2732x2048px) - at least 3 required

### App Preview Video (Optional but recommended)
- Up to 3 videos per device size
- 15-30 seconds each

## Google Play Store Requirements

### App Icon
- **Size**: 512x512px
- **Format**: PNG (32-bit)
- **Location**: `assets/images/adaptive-icon.png`

### Feature Graphic
- **Size**: 1024x500px
- **Format**: PNG or JPEG

### Screenshots Required
- Phone: At least 2 (1080x1920px or higher)
- 7" Tablet: At least 2 (1024x600px or higher)
- 10" Tablet: At least 2 (2048x1536px or higher)

## Privacy Policy
- **Required**: URL to hosted privacy policy
- **Recommended**: Host at https://mpb.health/privacy-policy
